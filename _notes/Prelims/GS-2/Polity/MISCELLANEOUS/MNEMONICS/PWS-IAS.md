### **Mnemonics for Indian Constitution**

Remembering the articles, parts, and amendments of the **Indian Constitution** can be overwhelming, but using mnemonics makes it easier. These mnemonics simplify key concepts and help you retain important details, especially for the **Polity section** of the UPSC exam. Here are some Mnemonics for Indian Constitution:

1. **Parts of the Constitution**:
    - **PARTS of Constitution: LAPS**
        - L: **Legislature**
        - A: **Amendment**
        - P: **Preamble**
        - S: **Supreme Court**
2. **Directive Principles of State Policy**:
    - **“GaMaDiGe” for the economic principles**:  
        **Ga**: Gandhian principles  
        **Ma**: Marxist principles  
        **Di**: Directive Principles  
        **Ge**: Gandhian Economic principles
3. **Fundamental Rights (Part III)**:
    - **Mnemonic**: **RITE**  
        **R**: Right to Equality (Articles 14-18)  
        **I**: Right to Freedom (Articles 19-22)  
        **T**: Right against Exploitation (Articles 23-24)  
        **E**: Right to Constitutional Remedies (Article 32)
